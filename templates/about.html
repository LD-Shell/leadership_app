About Leadership Assessment

This Leadership Assessment app was created by Olanrewaju Daramola to help individuals evaluate their leadership qualities in a modern, user-friendly format.

Using a series of carefully crafted questions and modern web technologies like Flask and Bootstrap, this application provides instant results and a detailed breakdown of leadership competencies.
